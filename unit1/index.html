Unit 1 Project

Instructions

Build a web page that contains the following elements:

  1. A title appropriate to the contect of the page.
  2. A page heading using the largest heading.
  3. At least one medium-sized heading (not the largest or smallest).
  4. At least two paragraph tags with content.
  5. At least one
    line break element.
  6. Some italizised text using HTML tags.
  7. Some bold text using HTML tags.
  8. An ordered list with at least three items.
  9. An unordered list with at least three items.
  10. at least two absolute hyperlinks.

To recieve credit for this assighment. All of these elements must be present and correct.

Content

The web page may contain any content appropriate for a school seting. Some examples include:

  • A page about your favorite band with members and albums.
  • A recipe page for your favorite meal with ingredients and steps.
  • A DIY project with materials and steps
